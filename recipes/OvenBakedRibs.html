Oven Baked Ribs

Description

Oven baked ribs that are insanely savory and not too sweet

Ingredients

  • 1 Rack of Ribs
  • 1 Cup of Siracha
  • 1 tbsp Salt
  • 1 tbsp Pepper
  • 2 tbsp Cumin Seeds
  • 1 tbsp Garlic Powder
  • 1 tbsp Onion Powder
  • 1 tbsp of Paprika
  • 1 tbsp of Cayenne Pepper
  • 1 tbsp of Oregano

    • Steps

    1. Combine salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, cumin, paprika, and oregano into a dry rub.
    2. Rub Siracha onto both sides of the ribs as a binder
    3. Spread the dry rub evenly along both sides of the ribs
    4. Bake in the oven at 300 degrees Fahrenheit for 20 minutes or until 160 degrees Fahrenheit internally
